Common Pain Points in Self-Integrated Carriers Process

Our analysis of seven recent integrations reveals that most issues fall into three major categories. Problems related to agreements and approvals are the most frequent cause of delays and project failure.

Breakdown of Self-Integrated Carrier Process Challenges

Agreement & Approval Issues

Finalizing contracts and getting internal approvals consistently emerge as the primary bottleneck, often occurring too late in the process.

Resource & Timeline Misalignment

Projects frequently stall due to unclear timelines, undefined support levels, and partners lacking the necessary resources to complete the integration.
